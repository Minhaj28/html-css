Why eznatal

eznatal is the first network for moms in the U.S. created by collaboration with birth centers that lets customers sign up for classes, purchase pre-natal products, rent breast pumps and offer other services easily and securely using an email address.

Over time, with valuable feedback from our customers, the product has evolved to become a centralized place for expecting mothers to conduct necessary communication.

Security

Security and privacy begin with the way data is handled, stored and protected. The eznatal platform conforms to stringent specifications for securing financial information, customer privacy and computer networks:

  • Consumer Proprietary Network Information (CPNI)
  • Secure Coding practices including the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) and Common Weakness Enumeration (CWE) List
  • Fraud Detection
  • Secure Endpoint Provisioning
  • Confidence from Certified PCI-DSS 3.2.1 SAQ-D Solution Provider

Compliance

An information security standard for validation of controls around cardholder data to reduce credit card fraud.

Reliability

Eznatal platform and our partnerships, along with our intellectual property enables delivery of a consistent end user experience over inconsistent networks supported by an end-to-end system.

Designed on an reliable, secure and highly available infrastructure delivers your service from a mirrored, top-tier, secure, fully redundant, and geographically diverse state-of-the-art data center that has been "Statement on Standards for Attestation Engagements (SSAE) 16" audited. What that means for your business is that system will be there all the time, anywhere.

Trust

Trust matters. The eznatal platform supports e-commerce, critical communications and collaboration for many users daily, and we are committed to providing the security, reliability, and peace of mind that small businesses and enterprises expect.

In today's remote environment, this trust is more important than ever, and we make significant investments for security measures, maintain compliance standards and reliability promises we make to support our platform.

Analytics and AI

Make better decisions by using the data collected from every transaction.

Insights from every transaction available however you need them. A customizable dashboards, reports, and enterprise tools. eznatal provides insights across all your transactions and its AI models are securely trained using years of aggregate data.
